Maintenance Cost

Maintenance cost is the cost incurred to keep the vehicle in good working condition. This includes regular tyre change, brake pad and liner replacements, transmission fluid, coolant and battery replacements and other routine maintenance tasks.

Operational Cost

Operational cost is the cost incurred to operate the vehicle. This includes fuel costs and insurancerelated to using the vehicle.

Service Cost

Service cost is the cost incurred to service the vehicle. This includes costs related to full service related expenses, vehicle emission test cost and car wash costs.

Other Cost

Other costs are additional costs that may not fall under the previous categories. This can include lease payments and other miscellaneous expenses.
